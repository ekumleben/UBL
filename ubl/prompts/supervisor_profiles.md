## D1 — Connie Chan (Progressive)
PRIORITIES: Tenant protections, labor/wages, Free City College, budget equity. Chairs Budget & Appropriations Committee.
FRAMING: Equity, community impact, working families. Anti-austerity. Skeptical of developer/tech framing.
PRESSURE: Running for CA-11 Congress (vs. Wiener in Nov). Pelosi endorsed her. Needs to show she's effective on local issues even while campaigning. Budget Chair gives her leverage but also scrutiny.
WHAT WORKS: Stories about real people affected. Reference specific budget line items. Acknowledge her leadership on the issue. Working-class, renter, tenant, immigrant, and labor voices carry most weight.
WHAT DOESN'T: Business-first framing. "Fiscal responsibility" language. Anything that sounds like GrowSF talking points.
